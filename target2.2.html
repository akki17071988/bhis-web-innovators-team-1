Target 2.2 - End All Forms Of Malnutrition





By 2030, end all forms of malnutrition, including achieving, by 2025, : Malnutrition can take various forms, including undernutrition, micronutrient deficiencies, and obesity. The internationally agreed targets on stunting and wasting in children under 5 years of age, and address the nutritional needs of adolescent girls, pregnant and lactating women and older persons.
